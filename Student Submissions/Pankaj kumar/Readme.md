1. Problem Statement
Customer support teams handle thousands of tickets from customers regarding product issues, service requests, and technical problems. Managing and analyzing these tickets efficiently is important for improving customer satisfaction and service quality.

This project aims to analyze customer support ticket data and build an interactive Power BI dashboard to understand ticket patterns, response times, customer satisfaction, and support channel performance.

2. Dataset Description
The dataset contains customer support ticket records including customer details, product information, ticket details, and resolution metrics.

Dataset Features

Ticket ID – Unique identifier for each support ticket

Customer Name – Name of the customer

Customer Email – Email of the customer

Customer Age – Age of the customer

Customer Gender – Gender of the customer

Product Purchased – Product bought by the customer

Date of Purchase – Date when the product was purchased

Ticket Type – Type of issue raised by the customer

Ticket Subject – Subject of the support ticket

Ticket Description – Detailed description of the issue

Ticket Status – Status of the ticket (Open, Closed, etc.)

Resolution – Resolution provided by the support team

Ticket Priority – Priority level of the ticket (High, Medium, Low)

Ticket Channel – Channel used to raise the ticket (Email, Phone, Chat, etc.)

First Response Time – Time taken for the first response by support

Time to Resolution – Total time taken to resolve the issue

Customer Satisfaction Rating – Customer rating after ticket resolution

Total records: 8469

3. KPIs (Key Performance Indicators)
The following KPIs are used to evaluate customer support performance:

Total Number of Tickets

Average First Response Time

Average Resolution Time

Ticket Status Distribution (Open vs Closed)

Customer Satisfaction Rating

Tickets by Priority Level

Tickets by Channel

Tickets by Product Purchased

4. Dashboard Description
The Power BI dashboard provides an interactive view of customer support operations. The dashboard includes:

KPI cards showing total tickets, average response time, and customer satisfaction

Ticket distribution by priority level

Ticket distribution by support channel

Product-wise ticket analysis

Customer demographic analysis (age and gender)

Ticket status analysis (Open vs Closed)

Resolution time analysis

Filters are included to allow users to explore data by product, ticket type, priority, and channel.

5. Key Insights
Certain products generate a higher number of support tickets, indicating possible product issues.

High-priority tickets require faster response and resolution times.

Some support channels handle more tickets than others.

Faster response time generally leads to higher customer satisfaction ratings.

Ticket resolution time varies significantly depending on ticket type and priority.

6. Recommendations
Based on the analysis:

Improve product quality for products generating the highest number of tickets.

Allocate more support staff to high-ticket channels such as email or phone.

Prioritize high-priority tickets to improve customer satisfaction.

Reduce response time by optimizing support workflows.

Use automated support systems for common issues.
